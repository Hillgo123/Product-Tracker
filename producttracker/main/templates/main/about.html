About

Welcome to Product Tracker, the ultimate platform for keeping an eye on your favorite products and their prices!

Product Tracker is designed to make it easy for you to monitor and track the prices of products from various online stores. By simply adding a product link to your tracking list, you'll be able to keep track of the latest prices and receive email notifications whenever a price change occurs.

Our mission is to help you save both time and money when shopping online. With Product Tracker, you no longer need to manually check the prices of the products you're interested in. Our platform does the heavy lifting for you, so you can make informed purchasing decisions and never miss out on a great deal.
